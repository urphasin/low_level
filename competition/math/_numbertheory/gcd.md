## Intuition

Each step reduces the problem:

gcd(a, b) = gcd(b, a mod b)

The integral of a function: $\int f(x)\,dx$

$$
\int f(x)\,dx
$$

$$
\int_{a}^{b} f(x)\,dx
$$


$$
\int_{0}^{1} x^2 \, dx
$$

$$
\iint_D f(x,y)\, dA
$$

$$
\iiint_V f(x,y,z)\, dV
$$

## Key Symbols
` 
\int → integral symbol
\iint, \iiint → multiple integrals
_ → lower limit
^ → upper limit
\, → small spacing (for readability)
`
